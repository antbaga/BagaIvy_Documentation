Visibility

The Visibility panel allows you to hide or display different parts of the ivy. This is primarily used to optimize viewport performance while working on heavy scenes or to isolate specific elements during the creation process.

  • Use Leaves: Toggle to show or hide all leaf instances in the viewport.
  • Use Flower: Toggle to show or hide all flowers and fruits.
  • Hide Core: Masks the main skeletal branches generated in Step 1.
  • Hide Foliage: Masks the smaller secondary branches generated in Step 2.
