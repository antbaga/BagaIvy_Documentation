# Cut Tool

The **Cut Tool** is a specialized utility available exclusively within **Step 2 - Foliage Growth**. It provides a way to manually trim/shorten secondary branches that may growing in unwanted areas.

---

## How to use the Cut Tool

1.  Navigate to the **Cut Tool** section within the Step 2 menu.
2.  For the first use, click the **Use Cut** button to initialize the trimming system.
3.  Click on the **Cut !** button. 
    * Blender will automatically switch to **Edit Mode** with the **Draw Tool** active.
4.  **Draw to Trim**: draw curves that intersect the secondary branches (the ones from Step 2) you wish to shorten. Branch will be cut at the point where they cross your drawn curve.

---

## Parameters

* **Distance**: Adjusts the detection range for intersections between your drawn curve and the branches.
* **Threshold**: Controls the sensitivity of the cut.
